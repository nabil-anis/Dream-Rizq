Today's Spiritual Rizq

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْعَفْوَ وَالْعَافِيَةَ

O Allah, I ask You for forgiveness and well-being.

May every tap of ‘Ameen’ bear witness for us on the Day of Judgment.

Powerful Duas

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

Transliteration: Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan waqina 'adhab an-nar


Our Lord, give us in this world [that which is] good and in the Hereafter [that which is] good and protect us from the punishment of the Fire.

[Qur'an 2:201]

اللّهُـمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّـي لا إلهَ إِلاّ أَنْتَ

Transliteration: Allahumma anta Rabbi la ilaha illa Anta


O Allah, You are my Lord, there is no deity except You.

[Sahih Bukhari 6306]

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَارْحَمْنِي وَاهْدِنِي وَعَافِنِي وَارْزُقْنِي

Transliteration: Rabbi ighfir li, warhamni, wahdini, wa 'afini, warzuqni


My Lord, forgive me, have mercy on me, guide me, grant me health, and provide me with sustenance.

[Sahih Muslim 2709]

اللّهُـمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الْفَقْرِ وَالْقِلَّةِ وَالذِّلَّةِ

Transliteration: Allahumma inni aʿudhu bika minal-faqri wal-qillati wadh-dhillah


O Allah, I seek refuge in You from poverty, scarcity, and humiliation.

[Sahih Bukhari 2823]

اللّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ فِي قَلْبِي نُورًا وَفِي لِسَانِي نُورًا

Transliteration: Allahumma ajʿal fi qalbi nuran wa fi lisani nuran


O Allah, place light in my heart and light upon my tongue.

[Sahih Muslim 763]

رَبِّ لَا تَذَرْنِي فَرْدًا وَأَنْتَ خَيْرُ الْوَارِثِينَ

Transliteration: Rabbi la tadharni fardan wa anta khayrul-warithin


My Lord, do not leave me alone [without offspring], while You are the best of inheritors.

[Qur'an 21:89]

اللّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الَّذِينَ سَمِعُوا الْقَوْلَ فَاتَّبَعُوا أَحْسَنَهُ

Transliteration: Allahumma ajʿalna mina alladhina samiʿu al-qawla fattabaʿu ahsanah


O Allah, make us among those who listen to the word and follow the best of it.

[Qur'an 39:18]

رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا

Transliteration: Rabbi zidni ʿilma


My Lord, increase me in knowledge.

[Qur'an 20:114]

سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ، سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ الْعَظِيمِ

Transliteration: Subhan Allahi wa bihamdihi, Subhan Allahil-ʿAzim


Glory is to Allah and all praise is to Him; glory is to Allah, the Most Great.

[Sahih Bukhari 6405; Sahih Muslim 2694]

حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ، عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ، وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ

Transliteration: Hasbiyallahu la ilaha illa Huwa, ʿalayhi tawakkaltu, wa Huwa Rabbul-ʿArshil-ʿAzim


Allah is sufficient for me; there is no deity except Him. In Him I have placed my trust, and He is the Lord of the Mighty Throne.

[Qur'an 9:129]

Transliteration: Allahumma inni aʿudhu bika minal-jooʿi wal-jazʿi


O Allah, I seek refuge in You from hunger and from anxiety.

[Sahih Muslim 1014]

اللّهُـمَّ اجْعَلْ فِي قَلْبِي رَحْمَةً وَفِي لِسَانِي رَحْمَةً

Transliteration: Allahumma ajʿal fi qalbi rahmatan wa fi lisani rahmatan


O Allah, place mercy in my heart and mercy upon my tongue.

[Sunan Ibn Majah 1/205]

Transliteration: Allahumma la tuʾakhidhni bima yaquluna wa ajilni khayran mimma yazunnun


O Allah, do not hold me accountable for what they say, and make me better than what they think of me.

[Sunan Abi Dawood 2/318]

اللّهُـمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ فِتْنَةِ الدُّنْيَا وَمِنْ شَرِّ فِتْنَةِ الْمَسِيحِ الدَّجَّالِ

Transliteration: Allahumma inni aʿudhu bika min sharri fitnati ad-dunya wa min sharri fitnati al-Masih ad-Dajjal


O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the evil of the trials of this world and from the evil of the trial of the false Messiah (Dajjal).

[Sahih Muslim 2937]

اللّهُـمَّ أَعِزَّ الْإِسْلَامَ وَأَعِزَّ الْمُسْلِمِينَ

Transliteration: Allahumma aʿizzil-Islama wa aʿizzil-Muslimeen


O Allah, honor Islam and honor the Muslims.

[Sunan Abi Dawood 2/312]

اللّهُـمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ رَحْمَتَكَ وَمَغْفِرَتَكَ وَعَافِيَتَكَ

Transliteration: Allahumma inni asʾaluka rahmataka wa maghfirataka wa ʿafiyataka


O Allah, I ask You for Your mercy, forgiveness, and health.

[Sunan At-Tirmidhi 3/171]

اللّهُـمَّ عَافِنِي فِي بَدَنِي وَعَافِنِي فِي سَمْعِي وَعَافِنِي فِي بَصَرِي

Transliteration: Allahumma aʿafini fi badani wa aʿafini fi samʿi wa aʿafini fi basari


O Allah, grant me health in my body, and in my hearing and in my sight.

[Sunan Ibn Majah 1/420]

اللّهُـمَّ اجْعَلْ قَلْبِي مُدْنِيًا لَكَ وَذُنُوبِي مَغْفُورَةً

Transliteration: Allahumma ajʿal qalbi mudnīyan laka wa dhunubi maghfuratan

[Sahih Muslim 2624]

O Allah, protect me from the punishment of Hell.

[Sahih Muslim 214]

اللّهُـمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ الْهُدَى وَالتُّقَى وَالْعَفَافَ وَالْغِنَى

Transliteration: Allahumma inni asʾaluka al-huda wa at-tuqa wal-ʿafafa wal-ghina

O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the punishment of Hell.

Transliteration: Rabbi ghfir li warhamni wahdini wajburni


My Lord, forgive me, have mercy on me, guide me, and mend my affairs.

[Sunan At-Tirmidhi 3527]

Transliteration: Allahumma rabbi a'udhu bika min sharri ma khalaqt

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي مِنَ الَّذِينَ يَقُولُونَ رَبُّنَا غَفَرْتَ لَنَا

O Allah, Lord, do not make me have sorrow in my heart.

O Allah, place light in my heart.

Pearls from the Prophet ﷺ

«إِنَّما الأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ وَإِنَّما لِكُلِّ امرِئٍ مَا نَوَى»

Transliteration: Innamā al-a‘mālu bin-niyyāti wa innamā likulli imri’imā nawwā


"Actions are judged by intentions, so each person will be rewarded according to what he intended."

[Sahih al-Bukhari 1]

«خَيْرُ النَّاسِ أَنْفَعُهُمْ لِلنَّاسِ»

Transliteration: Khayru an-nāsi anfa‘uhum li-n-nāsi


"The best of people are those that bring most benefit to the rest of mankind."

[Daraqutni, Hasan]

«مَن دَلَّ عَلَىٰ خَيْرٍ فَلَهُ مِثْلُ أَجْرِ فَاعِلِهِ»

Transliteration: Man dallā ‘alā khayrin falahu mithlu ajri fā‘ilihi


"Whoever shows you a good way, follow it, for the one who follows good deeds, his reward will be equal to that of the one who shows it."

[Sahih Muslim 1017]

«لَا تَحَاسَدُوا وَلَا تَبَاغَضُوا وَلَا تَدَابَرُوا وَكُونُوا عِبَادَ اللَّهِ إِخْوَانًا»

Transliteration: Lā taḥāsadū wa lā tabāghadū wa lā tadābarū wa kūnū ‘ibād Allāhi ikhwanā


"Do not envy one another. Do not hate one another. Do not turn your backs on one another. And do not undercut one another in trade. Be, O servants of Allah, brothers."

[Sahih Muslim 2566]

«لَا قُوَّةَ لِلرَّجُلِ فِي لِيَاقَتِهِ إِلَّا أَنْ يَحْتَسِي غَضَبَهُ»

Transliteration: Lā quwwata li-rajuli fī liyāqatihi illā an yaḥtasi ghadabahu


"The strong man is not the one who is able to wrestle, but the strong man is he who controls himself when he gets angry."

[Sahih al-Bukhari 6114]

«خَيْرُكُمْ أَحْسَنُكُمْ خُلُقًا»

Transliteration: Khayrukum aḥsanukum khuluqā


"The best among you are those who have the best manners and character."

[Sahih al-Bukhari 6119]

«إِذَا أَحَبَّ اللَّهُ عَبْدًا نَادَىٰ جِبْرِيلَ أَنْي أَحْبِبْ فُلَانًا فَيُحِبُّهُ جِبْرِيلُ»

Transliteration: Idhā aḥabba Allāhu ‘abdan nādā Jibrīl anī aḥibb fulānan fayuḥibbuhu Jibrīl


"When Allah loves a servant, He calls Jibril and says: ‘I love so-and-so, so love him.’ Then Jibril loves him, and makes an announcement in the heavens saying: ‘Allah loves so-and-so, so love him.’ And the inhabitants of the heavens love him. Then acceptance is placed for him on earth."

[Sahih Muslim 2637]

«مَنْ لَا يَشْكُرُ النَّاسَ لَا يَشْكُرُ اللَّهَ»

Transliteration: Man lā yashkuru an-nāsa lā yashkuru Allāh


"Whoever does not thank people, does not thank Allah."

[Sunan Abi Dawood 4811]

«خَيْرُكُمْ خَيْرُكُمْ لِأَزْوَاجِهِ»

Transliteration: Khayrukum khayrukum li-azwājih


"The best of you are those who are the best to their women."

[Sunan Ibn Majah 1854]

«مَن فَأَجَلَ حَاجَةَ أَخِيهِ فَفَأَجَلَ اللَّهُ حَاجَتَهُ»

Transliteration: Man fa’ajala ḥājata akhīhi fa-fa’ajala Allāhu ḥājatahu


"Whoever fulfills the needs of his brother, Allah will fulfill his needs."

[Sahih al-Bukhari 2442]

«إِذَا لَمْ تَسْتَحِي فَفَعَلَ مَا شِئْتَ»

Transliteration: Idhā lam tastahī fa-fa‘al mā shi’t


"If you do not feel ashamed, then do as you wish."

[Sahih al-Bukhari 6120]

«لَا يُؤْمِنُ أَحَدُكُمْ حَتَّى يُحِبَّ لِأَخِيهِ مَا يُحِبُّ لِنَفْسِهِ»

Transliteration: Lā yu’minu aḥadukum ḥattā yuḥibbu li-akhīhi mā yuḥibbu linafsihi


"A believer is not the one who eats his fill while his neighbor goes hungry."
